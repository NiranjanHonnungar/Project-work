WHO ARE WE?

Started in 2020 with a passion and love for anything sweet, we put in our experience and knowledge of baking into action and the birth of the Sweet Sphere took place.

Keeping in mind our pure vegetarian policy, we provide a diverse range of eggless desserts made with love and perfection.

Our entire menu is made fresh daily by our dessert enthusiasts, committed to preserving the art and authencity of pastry making while taking a modern approach.

WHAT WE DO

We specialise in eggless baking and bring to you mouth watering delicacies for all the sweet tooths out there. We are committed to using fresh, high-end and local ingredients and only serve the best.

Here, at The Sweet Sphere , we serve in smaller batches giving more attention to each dessert, providing top quality desserts of a wide range. So whether it's a birthday, dinner party, wedding or shower, let us help you make your event extra special!

WHERE WE ARE LOCATED

We are a bakery that working out of a small flat in Indirinagar, Bangalore. We are a group of 5 friends who are based in Bangalore.
